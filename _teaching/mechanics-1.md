This bachelor's course was offered at the University of Stuttgart with C. David Remy as the main lecturer. The teaching overview records a cohort of about 60 students for the `winter 2022` offering.

## Course Description

This introductory course is aimed at first-semester bachelor's students in Renewable Energies and Transport Engineering. After a short introduction to vector algebra, it covers the kinematics and statics of rigid bodies, equilibrium under external loads, statically equivalent load cases, centers of mass, trusses and beams, and basic deformation of tension and compression members.
