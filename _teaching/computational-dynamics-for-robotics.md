## Course Description

Computational Dynamics for Robotics introduces the kinematics and dynamics of multibody systems arising in robotics, mechatronics, and biomechanics. The course develops precise mathematical models and efficient formulations for deriving governing differential equations, with particular emphasis on physical intuition, collisions and variable structure, and algorithmic implementations. In the exercises, students implement a complete multibody dynamics engine in MATLAB using recursive methods and object-oriented programming.
