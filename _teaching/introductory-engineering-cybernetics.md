This course is currently taught at the University of Stuttgart with C. David Remy as the main lecturer. I contributed to the development of class content and a hardware exercise for the course.

## Course Description

This course offers an accessible introduction to engineering cybernetics by familiarizing students with the core ideas of modeling, simulation, systems theory, and control through simple examples and hands-on hardware experiments. In doing so, it provides a foundation for the more advanced courses taught in the following semesters.

## Details

- German title: `Einführung in die Technische Kybernetik`
- Main lecturer: C. David Remy
- Institution: University of Stuttgart
- Typical cohort size: about 30 students
